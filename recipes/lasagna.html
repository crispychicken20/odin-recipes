Lasagna:

Final Product

Description:

Lasagna is a delicacy originating from italy, you might have heard it from the cartoon of the cat that loves lasagna.

Making lasagna is not as difficult as you think it can actually be quite simple behind the process. The ingredients and steps will be listed to assist you throughout the process of making lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • Meat:
  • Oninion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
